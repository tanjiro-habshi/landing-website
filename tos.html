Terms of Service

WHAT DATA DO WE STORE?

  • We prioritize the protection of user privacy and do not collect any personal information such as passwords or other credentials. The data we do collect comprises User IDs, Server IDs, Channel IDs, and select Role IDs. It's crucial to note that our bot never solicits personal data from users, and we actively discourage any actions that contravene Discord's Terms of Service.

WHY WE NEED THIS DATA?

  • The data is required for the proper functioning of the bot features like Warning System, Logging and Autoroles. Without this data, our bot will not be able to perform these activities and thus making the features inaccessible for users

HOW DO WE USE THIS DATA?

  • The data is used for the proper functioning for the Warning System, Logging activities and Autorole features of our Bot. User IDs are used to identify the users, Channel IDs are used to send the messages to the desginated channels and Server IDs to identify the Servers and the Role IDs are used for the Autorole feature

HOW LONG DO WE STORE YOUR DATA?

  • The data is stored as long as the bot is in your Server and as soon as the bot is kicked or removed from the Server, the data is deleted and is no longer to accessable to anyone

WHO DO WE SHARE THE DATA WITH?

  • We never share your data with anyone except for MongoDB Inc whom is a DataBase Company and all the data for our Bot is stored on the MongoDB Servers and According to MongoDB Inc. the data is only accessable by us.

GOT CONCERNS OR QUERIES?

  • If you have any concerns or queries relating our privacy policy or our bot or if you want your data to be removed, You can contact tanjiro_habshi directy on our Support Server!
